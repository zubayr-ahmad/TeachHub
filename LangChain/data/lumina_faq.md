# Lumina Pixel — Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I reset Pixel?**
Press and hold the power button on the top of the device for 10 seconds until the light
ring pulses white. This performs a soft reset without erasing your settings.

**Can Pixel climb stairs?**
No. Pixel is designed for single-floor operation and will detect and stop at the edge of a
staircase. For multi-floor homes, many owners buy one Pixel per floor.

**Is a subscription required?**
No. All core features work offline and without payment. The optional Lumina Cloud plan
(4.99 US dollars per month) only adds cloud backup and remote camera access.

**What languages does Pixel understand?**
Pixel One understands English and Spanish. Pixel Pro understands 12 languages, including
English, Spanish, French, German, Urdu, Hindi, Arabic, Mandarin, Japanese, Portuguese,
Italian, and Korean.

**How long does the battery last?**
Pixel One lasts about 8 hours of active use; Pixel Pro lasts about 12 hours. Both reach a
full charge in roughly 3 hours on the dock.

**How do I contact support?**
