# Lumina Robotics — Product & Company Handbook

## About Lumina Robotics

Lumina Robotics was founded in 2019 by Dr. Ayesha Khan. The company is headquartered in
Austin, Texas, and builds friendly home-assistant robots. Its mission is to make helpful
robotics affordable for ordinary households.

## The Pixel home robot

Pixel is Lumina's flagship product: a compact home-assistant robot that helps with
reminders, home monitoring, and simple voice tasks. Pixel navigates a single floor of a
home using LiDAR and a depth camera, and it is designed to be safe around children and
pets.

There are two models:

- **Pixel One** — the standard model, priced at 499 US dollars. It understands English
  and Spanish.
- **Pixel Pro** — the premium model, priced at 899 US dollars. It adds a 4K camera, an
  extended 12-hour active battery, and voice understanding in 12 languages.

## Technical specifications (shared)

- Weight: 2.3 kilograms
- Maximum movement speed: 1.2 meters per second
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.2
- Sensors: LiDAR, a depth camera, and six far-field microphones
- Charging: returns to a USB-C dock and reaches a full charge in about 3 hours

### Battery life

Pixel One provides about 8 hours of active use and up to 30 days of standby on a single
charge. Pixel Pro extends active use to about 12 hours.

## Software and updates

Pixel receives free over-the-air software updates every month. Updates install
automatically overnight while Pixel is docked. The current major software version is
Pixel OS 4.

## Privacy

By default, all voice and sensor data is processed and stored locally on the device.
Cloud sync is optional and turned off unless the owner enables it. Lumina never sells
customer data.

## Warranty and returns

Every Pixel includes a 24-month limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects.
Customers may return a Pixel within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided the
device is returned in its original packaging.

## Subscriptions

Pixel works fully without any subscription. An optional service, **Lumina Cloud**, costs
4.99 US dollars per month and adds encrypted cloud backup of reminders and remote access
to Pixel's camera while away from home.

## Customer support

Support is available by email at support@luminarobotics.example. The support team
operates Monday through Friday, from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. Central Standard Time. Typical email
response time is one business day.
